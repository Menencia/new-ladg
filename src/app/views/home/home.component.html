Welcome to The Grandshelt Archives

This website's purpose is to preserve the mobile game Final Fantasy Brave Exvius after its End of Service took place on the 30th october 2024. All story mode cutscenes, earlier story events, summaries and more can be found here. Everything was made by a dedicated team of fans to allow you to discover or rediscover the incredibly deep lore and captivating story of this game. You can either watch the story videos edited in episodes and chapters or read the illustrated summaries of each part when it's available. The design was based on both the Room of Recollection (FFBE replay mode) and FFBE Ultimania (only available in Japan).

We hope you have a nice trip… As Knights of Grandshelt !

The Grandshelt Archives Team
